# Simplified Chinese Theme And Color Translation Rules

Use this note when the Simplified Chinese task covers theme titles, palette names, dual-tone descriptions, accessibility labels, or custom color import/export UI.

## Theme Titles

- Write theme titles as natural Simplified Chinese product names.
- Avoid stiff word-for-word calques when a smoother Simplified Chinese title works.
- Keep titles compact enough for a settings picker.
- Use normal UI wording rather than decorative English headline casing.

## Dual-Tone Descriptions

- Keep dual-tone descriptions short and color-led.
- Prefer compact color-list phrasing such as `色 A、色 B。` or `色 A、色 B、色 C。`
- Do not turn the description line into lore copy, atmosphere prose, or marketing narration.
- Avoid repeating `双色调调色盘`, `外观`, or similar framing words when the theme context is already obvious in UI.

## Accessibility Labels

- Prefer a clear pattern such as `名称主题，颜色 ...`
- Keep color names explicit and easy to scan in screen-reader output.
- Accessibility text should describe the visible palette directly, without decorative expansion.

## Material Palette Names

- Keep `Material` unchanged as the design-system family name.
- Use natural Simplified Chinese color names when they sound normal in a palette picker.
- Keep palette labels compact and product-like.

## Product-Term Consistency

- Keep `双色调` as the stable product/system term when the UI is naming the product option.
- Prefer `自定义` for user-created themes and palettes.
- In import/export UI, prefer short Simplified Chinese product wording over literal expansion.
