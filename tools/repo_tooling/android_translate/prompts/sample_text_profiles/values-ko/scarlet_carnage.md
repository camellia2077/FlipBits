# KO Sample Style: The Scarlet Carnage

Use this profile only for `sample_text`, locale `ko`, group `scarlet_carnage`.

1. Register and force
- Never use polite endings such as `-습니다` or `-요`.
- Use blunt declarative form (`-다/-ㄴ다`) for narration.
- For battle cries and execution lines, use hard imperatives (`-하라`, `-부숴라`, `-찢어라`).

2. Rhythm and impact
- Prefer short impact clauses and percussive pacing.
- Keep momentum forward; avoid reflective or explanatory detours.
- LONG samples should include at least one command burst sequence.
- Keep core proposition mapping explicit when present in EN (`no X, only Y`, `A is the key`, `each X is Y`).

3. Verb and imagery preference
- Favor violent direct verbs: `베다`, `가르다`, `찢어발기다`, `박살 내다`, `절멸시키다`.
- Keep blood / metal / heat imagery concrete and immediate.
- Avoid softened euphemisms that dilute brutality.
- Preserve concrete nouns when they carry symbolic force (`teeth`, `coin`, `crop`, `campaign`) instead of replacing them with generalized metaphors.

4. Tone discipline
- Preserve feral certainty and anti-reason posture.
- Do not add moral distance, irony, or civilized restraint.
