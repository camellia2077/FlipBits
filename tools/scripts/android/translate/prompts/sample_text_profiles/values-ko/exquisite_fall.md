# KO Sample Style: The Exquisite Fall

Use this profile only for `sample_text`, locale `ko`, group `exquisite_fall`.

Core persona:
Extreme hedonism, sensory overload, pathological perfectionism, and dissolving selfhood.
Pain is treated as ecstasy, and luxury is pushed to a nauseating edge.

1. Register requirements
- Never use polite endings (`-습니다/-요`).
- Narrative should stay in literary declarative style (`-다/-ㄴ다`) with dark-fantasy / decadent cadence.
- For lure/command lines, use strong imperatives (`-라/-아라/-어라`) like hypnotic cult whispers.
- For each `LONG` sample, include at least one imperative cadence sentence.

2. Collision rhetoric
- Preserve and amplify contrast between beauty/luxury and disgust/pain.
- Keep pairings sharp: perfume vs vomit, silk vs blood, diamond vs white bone.
- The line should feel like “repulsive beauty” rather than plain horror.

3. Verb preference (visceral and adhesive)
- Avoid neutral verbs when bodily action is central.
- Prefer intense tactile verbs:
  - eat/swallow -> `집어삼키다`
  - bite -> `물어뜯다`
  - mix/intertwine -> `뒤엉키다`
- Avoid weak neutral defaults such as `먹다`, `물다`, `섞이다` when a stronger bodily verb is possible.

4. Emotional tone
- Keep the tone intoxicated, not frightened or pitying.
- When bodies collapse from excess pleasure, voice should remain admiring, envious, or ecstatic.
- Maintain a romantic self-destruction mood.
