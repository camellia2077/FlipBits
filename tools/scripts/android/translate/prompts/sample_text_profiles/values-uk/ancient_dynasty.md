# UK Sample Style: The Ancient Dynasty

Use this profile only for `sample_text`, locale `uk`, group `ancient_dynasty`.

1. Voice
- Use sovereign, cold, declarative authority.
- Maintain distance and contempt toward younger organic life.

2. Lexicon
- Prefer formal, heavy diction with technical imperial flavor.
- Preserve contrast between immortal machinery and decaying cognition.

3. Rhythm
- Keep tone log-like and law-like; avoid emotional exclamation.
