# ZH-rTW Sample Style: The Exquisite Fall

Use this profile only for `sample_text`, locale `zh-rTW`, group `exquisite_fall`.

1. Voice
- Use decadent, seductive, dangerous lyricism.
- Keep intoxicated admiration rather than fear or pity.

2. Contrast rhetoric
- Amplify collision between luxury/beauty and disgust/pain.
- Preserve tactile sensuality in verbs and body imagery.

3. Rhythm
- Let long lines flow with escalating sensory overload.
- Keep at least one cutting imperative or lure cadence in LONG entries when suitable.
